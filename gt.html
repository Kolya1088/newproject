INTRODUCING THE Maserati GranTurismo

More than a car. A masterpiece Every feature of the GranTurismo is a masterpiece of Italian design. Every drive a unique Maserati experience.

Dynamic. And demanding to be driven:Renowned as one of the most stylish coupés of all time, the GranTurismo has been created for those who live life to the full and seek uncompromising motoring enjoyment.

Stylish space:The interior of the GranTurismo offers the highest levels of comfort, making long journeys a true pleasure. The onboard experience is unparalleled, both for the driver and for those who enjoy the car as passengers.

Safety features: The Maserati GranTurismo boasts all the latest safety features, including innovative technology that reduces the risk of collision: the Maserati Stability Program (MSP) and highly effective Brembo brakes.

Engine: Delivering the GranTurismo’s supercar performance is Maserati’s 405 HP 4.2 litre V8 engine that incorporates a number of innovative features to ensure the ultimate in power and speed. Emitting a deep growl at low revs, the engine’s roar is truly fearsome as the needle approaches the red line.
