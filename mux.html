INTRODUCING THE ISUZU MU-X

A true family-sized seven-seat SUV, the Isuzu MU-X redefines the boundaries of adventure. Whether you’re navigating the weekday school run or traversing the wildest weekend, MU-X has you covered for the routine, extreme, and everything in between.

  • Seven-seat comfort: Whether you're doing a sports run or heading for adventure, the 7-seat Isuzu MU-X keep your family entertained.
  • 3 tonne towing: MU-X’s powerful Isuzu turbo diesel engine gives you an impressive 3 tonnes+ of towing capacity.
  • Off-road adventure: With the Terrain Command 4x4 system and robust yet refined suspension, you can drive MU-X into the toughest terrain with ease.
  • Fuel efficient SUV: MU-X has a highly-efficient Isuzu diesel engine that uses leading technology to provide precise fuel control.
