INTRODUCING THE ISUZU D-MAX

The award-winning ute designed by the world’s largest commercial vehicle manufacturer. All the refinements you need to ride in comfort, but with Isuzu DNA, D-MAX is built tough to get the job done.

  • Go Further. Spend less:D-MAX has a highly-efficient Isuzu diesel engine that uses leading technology to provide precise fuel control, so it does more work and burns less fuel — saving you money.
  • 3.5 tonne towing:D-MAX’s powerful diesel engine is built for towing. It will pull trailers, boats and caravans up hill and down the highway without breaking a sweat.
  • Go where you want: Whether you’re going off-road for work or for fun, D-MAX will get you there and back.
  • 5-Star Safety: With a maximum 5-star safety rating on 4x4 Crew and 4x2 Crew High Ride models you can have complete peace of mind.
